# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of experience level, gender identity, race, religion, or nationality.

## Our Standards
- Use welcoming and inclusive language
- Respect differing viewpoints
- Accept constructive criticism gracefully
- Focus on what is best for the community
- Show empathy towards other community members

## Enforcement
Instances of abusive behavior may be reported to project maintainers via GitHub Issues.
